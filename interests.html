Interests

Philosophy

My interests primarily lie in ethics and religion. I love delving into the works of various philosophers, their ideologies, and teachings. I consider philosophy to be one of the most important forms of knowledge (since one of its primary goals is to literally teach us how to live).

Tennis

I've been playing tennis ever since I was a kid, and I love watching/playing it even today. I even wrote that I wanted to grow up to win a Grand Slam in my childhood report card. While my current favorite is Jannik Sinner, watching Federer play remains one of my favorite pastimes.

Reading

It is severely understated how much books can influence once lives. While I love reading Classic Literature, most books have a lot to offer since they give us insight into another person's psyche and what they were going through at a particular point in time.

Hi, I'm Adarsh Pai.

I’m a Mechanical Engineering student interested in fluid–structure interaction, unsteady aerodynamics, and bio-inspired design. Most of my work revolves around understanding how flexible or morphing structures interact with flow, whether it’s flapping foils, fish-like propulsion, or vortex-induced vibrations. I enjoy building things as much as modeling them, and I’m currently exploring how simple design changes can shift wake dynamics and improve performance in real systems.
